## Detox Melon Smoothie

*(1 person)*


*(6-7 person)*

**Ingredients:**

- 1 medium-size carrot
- 1 slice melon
- Peeled ginger in preferred amount
- 125 ml sparkling or spring water
- 4 ice cubes

**Directions:**

-  Add grated carrot, sliced melon, ginger and water to the blender. Then add ice cubes to blender for the second round.

Bon Appétit:)

p.s. You can adjust ginger size as you like.

Time: Ready in 5 minutes. :)
